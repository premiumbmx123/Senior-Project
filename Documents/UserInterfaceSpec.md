- Home Page (/)
  - Header(Title, Parts, Build Guide, Benchmarks)
  - Row of 5 of the best deals
    - Image of the part
    - Benchmark rank
    - Microcenter Link
    - Microcenter Price
    - Newegg Link
    - Newegg Price

  - Header(Title, Parts, Build Guide, Benchmarks)

    - Newegg Price
